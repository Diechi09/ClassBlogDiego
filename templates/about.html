About Fantasy Forum

Fantasy Forum is a community for NFL fantasy football discussion—trade help, waiver targets, injury talk, and more. It’s lightweight, quick, and focused on practical advice so you can win your league.

Under the hood: Flask + SQLite with auth, CRUD posts, comments, flairs, and a REST API (with export + live demo).

What you can do here

  • Post questions with a flair: TRADE HELP, WAIVER WIRE ADVICE, INJURY TALK, OTHER.
  • Comment with reasoning, data, and start/sit logic.
  • Edit/Delete your posts to keep info current.
  • Filter by flair on the Home page to find what you need fast.
  • Explore the API Demo or pull JSON from /api/posts.
  • Download all posts via JSON export.
